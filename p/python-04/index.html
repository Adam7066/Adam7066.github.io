Python-04：多線程-ts下載並合併成mp4

Oct 04, 2020

簡介

  這篇將帶你下載m3u8檔並分析出ts的檔案，再透過多線程來加速下載，之後再由FFmpeg合併成mp4。

Python

下載m3u8

1m3u8Url = 'https://.../index.m3u8'
2
3def downloadM3u8(url):
4    r = requests.get(url)
5    with open('./index.m3u8', 'wb') as f:
6        f.write(r.content)

分析m3u8

  • 這部份請依照你所取得的m3u8檔進行分析，並將完整的ts檔的url放進tsList即可。
 1tsList = []
 2tsCnt = 0
 3
 4def analyzeM3u8():
 5    tsList.clear()
 6    tempUrl = m3u8Url.rsplit('/', 1)[0] + '/'
 7    with open('./index.m3u8', 'r') as f:
 8        text = f.read()
 9    textList = text.split('\n')
10    while textList[-1] != '#EXT-X-ENDLIST':
11        textList.pop(-1)
12    for i in textList:
13        if i[0] != '#':
14            tsList.append(tempUrl + i)
15    global tsCnt 
16    tsCnt = len(tsList)

下載ts檔

 1q = queue.Queue()
 2
 3def downloadts():
 4    global tsList
 5    while q.qsize() > 0:
 6        num = q.get()
 7        r = requests.get(tsList[num])
 8        with open('./ts/' + str(num+1) + '.ts', 'wb') as f:
 9            f.write(r.content)
10
11if __name__ == "__main__":
12    if not os.path.isdir('./ts/'):
13        os.mkdir('./ts/')
14    for i in range(tsCnt):
15        q.put(i)
16    thList = []
17    for i in range(tsCnt):
18        th = threading.Thread(target=downloadts)
19        th.start()
20        thList.append(th)
21    for th in thList:
22        th.join()
23    print('下載ts完成')

合併ts成mp4

1def merge(tsCnt, output):
2    for i in range(tsCnt):
3        with open('./ts/ts.txt', 'a+') as f:
4            f.write('file ' + str(i+1) + '.ts\n')
5    command = 'ffmpeg -y -f concat -i %s -bsf:a aac_adtstoasc -c copy %s' % ('./ts/ts.txt', output)
6    os.system(command)
7    print('合併成功')

移除無用檔案
